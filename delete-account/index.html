Delete Your Vibly Account

This page explains how Vibly users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your Vibly account, send an email to:

Email: vibly.support@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Vibly account and mention that you want your account deleted.

Data that will be deleted

After your deletion request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User account information
  • Profile information
  • User-generated content connected to your account
  • Account authentication data

Data retention

Some information may be temporarily retained if required for security, legal obligations, fraud prevention, or technical reasons. Any retained information will be deleted after the required retention period.

Contact

For questions regarding account deletion, contact:

vibly.support@gmail.com
